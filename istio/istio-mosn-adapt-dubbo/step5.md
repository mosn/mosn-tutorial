## Consumer's Log

Look at Consumer running log: 

- `export CONSUMER_POD=$(kubectl get pod -n dubbo-app -l app=consumer -o jsonpath='{ .items[*].metadata.name }')`{{execute}}
- `kubectl logs -f -n dubbo-app $CONSUMER_POD -c consumer`{{execute}}
